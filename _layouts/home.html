What is Innovation OnBoard?

Innovation OnBoard is a FREE 6-month program filled with entrepreneurship seminars, workshops and a Dragons Den-style competition to foster innovation in engineering. UBC and SFU students from all faculties and departments are welcomed.

Register your team today for a chance to network with BC entrepreneurs and win more than $10,000 in prizes and sponsorship.

Innovation Themes

Our 2017 program focuses on three main themes in engineering: Health, Energy & Environment and Food.

  • Health

Health covers a wide range of topics including biotechnology and pharmaceuticals. Examples include medical devices, implants and any form of technology that targets diseases or increases a patient's quality of life.

  • Energy & Environment

With recent developments in the field of cleantech, energy and environment are increasingly intertwined. Innovations in this field include energy storage technology, processes that convert waste to value-added materials and renewable energy.

  • Food

Food security is an important topic globally. Entrepreneurship in this field include improvements in agricultural efficiency, strategies for reducing food wastage and innovative clean water technology.
